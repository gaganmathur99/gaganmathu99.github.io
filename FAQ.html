FAQ's

What kind of space is needed for the event?

When planning an event with Battle Balls you’ll need a gym free of benches and/or other climbing apparatus’.

Battle Balls can also be used outside on a soccer field provided it’s well-groomed without rocks or other debris. Turf fields also work great for Battle Ball events.

How much does it cost?

Prices on average are $15/person.

Is there a deposit?

A $75 deposit is required when the event is booked.

What happens if I have to cancel my event?

We will happily refund your deposit if you provide more than 48 hours notice prior to your event. However, if you cancel with less than 48 hours notice, your deposit is non-refundable.

Can I get hurt?

The equipment has been extensively tested for the games it is designed for. Like any sport we recommend using your best judgement while playing as you get comfortable; take proper safety precautions and wear supportive athletic shoes that you are comfortable running in.

What material are Battle Balls made of?

Battle Balls are made of TPU Plastic. These Thermoplastic Polyurethane balls are stronger and more resistant than their PVC counterparts.

Is there a height/weight limit?

The height limit is between 3’6 and 6’6. We recommend a maximum weight of 200 lbs.

Is there an age limit?

Children 6 and up. Although the limiting factor is height (Over 3’6). Children under 18 must be supervised at all times.
