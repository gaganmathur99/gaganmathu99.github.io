FAQ's

Where can Bubble Ball be played?

Bubble ball can be played at any flat field without any debris, for example, a well-maintained football field. It can also be played indoors, for example, a gymnasium hall of a sufficient size.

What is the price per person?

Price on average $25 per person. Also, It depends on the package you choose.

Is there a deposit?

Yes, 20% of total amount is required to reserve a slot.

Is my deposit amount refundable?

Yes, your prepayment is refundable if you cancel the event at least 48 hours before it starts.

Is it safe to play Bubble Ball?

It is absolutely safe to play Bubble Ball as the equipment is tried and tested. We recommend you to wear comfortable sport shoes.

Is there any height requirements?

You should at least be 3’6 and should not be more than 6’6.

Is there any weight limit?

We recommend the maximum weight of 200 lbs.

Is there any age requirements?

Basically children 7 and up but Although, we recommend that children under 18 years of age should play under supervision.

What are Bubble balls made of?

Our bubble balls are made of TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane ). TPU is higher quality material and they are more flexible allowing for more bounce.
