Rishav Sharma

“Rishav Sharma is the young and dynamic badminton enthusiast, whose brainchild is the Peace Region Badminton and Recreational Club. He has a number of medals to his name, which he won while representing Canada in various of International Competitions. His forte lies in promoting the Badminton as a sport by introducing it in the areas it is little known. That is the reason he set up a Badminton Club in his home, Fort Saint John, where he felt that the young ones need to be made familiar with the game. His efforts proved fruitful when five of his students from Fort Saint John participated in the BC Winter Games 2020. According to him, its just a start and he has great visions for the future.

Apart from promoting badminton, he has a belief that in an era of technology and screen-oriented lives, it is of utmost importance that recreation and sports go hand in hand. In furtherance of this belief, Mr. Sharma has introduced first of its kind sports in the Peace Region, the Bubble Ball. Once again, according to him, its just a start and he has a number of other activities planned for the citizens of the Peace Region”
